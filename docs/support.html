Bond Support

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Bond is a couples' love-language reminder app for iPhone and Apple Watch. Create reminders, track milestones, and stay connected with your partner.

Support contact
Email: jackwallner@gmail.com

Getting Started

  • Sign in with Apple — one tap, no passwords required.
  • Create an invite link and share it with your partner.
  • Once paired, you can create reminders and track milestones together.

Troubleshooting

  • If reminders aren't showing up for your partner, confirm you're both signed in and paired.
  • If push notifications aren't arriving, check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > Notifications > Bond.
  • If pairing fails, generate a new invite link from the Pairing screen and try again.
  • If the app isn't loading your data, sign out and sign back in to refresh your session.

Privacy

Bond uses Sign in with Apple for authentication and Supabase for secure storage. No ads, no third-party analytics, no data sharing. Your reminders and milestones are between you and your partner.
